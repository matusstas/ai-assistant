# COMPONENT ACCESS (CONT.)

## Agitator
1. Disconnect the power from washer.
2. Open Lid.
3. Pull up on agitator cap to remove.

4. Use a flat blade screwdriver to depress the two (2) tabs on the bottom piece of the fabric softener dispenser.

5. Lift dispenser bottom up and away to remove.

6. Remove the 5/16" bolt and washer from the cam assembly.

7. Lift cam assembly to remove.

8. Lift agitator auger to remove.

9. Use Phillips screwdriver to remove two (2) screws from the agitator couple.

10. Use flat blade screwdriver to pry tabs to release agitator couple. Lift to remove.
