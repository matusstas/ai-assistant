## Components Accessible under Top Panel

**WARNING**

Electrical Shock Hazard  
Disconnect power before servicing.  
Replace all parts and panels before operating.  
Failure to do so can result in death or electrical shock.

1. Disconnect the power from washer.  
2. Complete the steps of removing the Console and Components.  
3. Remove two (2) T20 security screws from the front edge.  

4. There are plastic bushings behind these screws that help avoid damage to the finish of the top. These bushings have clips securing them inside the top.  
5. Grasp the front edge of the top. Pull the top forward and lift to release it from the clips on the cabinet.  

6. A method to support the top will be necessary. A string with hooks on either end, or a prop rod can be used.
