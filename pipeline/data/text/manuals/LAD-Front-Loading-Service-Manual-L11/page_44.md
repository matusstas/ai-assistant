### Check Procedure:

#### Step 2: Replace the motor
1. Disassemble the 4 screws of the rear cover. (Fig.1)  
2. Remove the rear cover and then remove the belt. (Fig.2)  
3. After the soft things are on the floor mat, the washing machine is dumped forward and downward.  
4. Disassemble the screw on the rear tub and remove the terminal connecting the motor. (Fig.2)  
5. Take out the motor upwards and replace it with a new one.
