# 8 UNPACKING WAYS OF MAIN PARTS

### Undo the drum tub
I. Remove the screws fixing the pulley.  
II. Remove the screws fixing the front and rear tub, and then remove the tub.  
III. Remove the inner drum kit.  

### 13. Undo the absorber from the drum
I. Lift out the outer tub kit.  
II. Undo the absorber pin between absorber and rear tub, remove the absorber.  

### 14. Undo the heater and NTC
I. Pull out the heater plug.  
II. Pull out the NTC.

In the red circle it is the heater support, clamping the heater.
