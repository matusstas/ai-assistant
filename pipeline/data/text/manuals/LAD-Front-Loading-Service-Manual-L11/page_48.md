Check whether the harness connecting the wire to the motor is installed in place, and whether the harness connecting the wire to the main PCB is not installed in place

2. Push back the top cover plate 15mm until it leaves away from the control panel and then take it down. (Fig.2)
3. Check whether the harness connecting the wire to the main PCB is installed in place. (Fig.3)
4. Remove the 4 screws of the rear cover, and show the motor. (Fig.4 & Fig.5)
5. Check whether the harness connecting the wire to the motor is installed in place. (Fig.6)
