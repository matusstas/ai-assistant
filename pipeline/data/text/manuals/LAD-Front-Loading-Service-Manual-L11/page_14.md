5. MALFUNCTION CODES AND EXPLANATIONS

E10
- Check Procedure:

**Step 1** Check the water tap
1. Check the water tap and open it fully if it is not. (Fig.1)

**Step 2** Check the inlet filter
1. Close the tap and remove the water supply hose. (Fig.2)  
2. Clean the filter with a brush if it is blocked. (Fig.3)  
3. Unscrew the water supply hose from the backside of the machine. Pull out the filter with long nose pliers. (Fig.4)  
4. Clean the filter with a brush if it is blocked. (Fig.5)  

**Step 3** Check the water inlet valve
1. Close the tap and disassemble the 2 screws of the top cover plate. (Fig.6)  
2. Push back the top cover plate 15mm until it leaves away from the control panel and then take it down. (Fig.7)  
3. Measure the resistance value of the water inlet valve, 3-6kÎ© is OK. (Fig.8)  
4. If the resistance value of the water inlet valve is not well, change the water inlet valve. (Fig.9)
