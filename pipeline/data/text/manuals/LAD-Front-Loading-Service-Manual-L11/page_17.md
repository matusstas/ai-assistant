5. MALFUNCTION CODES AND EXPLANATIONS

E12
- Check Procedure:
  
  - Restart the machine to see if it still alarms
    - No: The machine is ok
    - Yes:
      - Whether the water is in without power
        - No:
          - Whether the air pipe or the connection is leaking
            - Yes: replace the air pipe or the connection
            - No:
              - Whether the water level sensor is well
                - Yes:
                  - Check the inner wire between PCB and the water level sensor, well or not
                    - No: replace the inner wire
                    - Yes: Replace the PCB.
                - No: replace the water level sensor
