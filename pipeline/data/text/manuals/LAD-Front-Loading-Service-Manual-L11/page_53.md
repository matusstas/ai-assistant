# 6 TROUBLESHOOTING

## 2. Door non-locked & its maintenance

**Door non-locked**

- Whether the door is closed correctly
  - **Y**
    - Whether the inner wire between the PCB and the door lock is correct
      - **Y**
        - Check the door lock is well, or not
          - **Y**
            - Change the PCB
          - **N**
            - Change the door lock
      - **N**
        - Connect the PCB and door lock correctly
  - **N**
    - Close the door correctly
