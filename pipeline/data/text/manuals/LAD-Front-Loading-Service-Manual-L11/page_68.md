# 8 UNPACKING WAYS OF MAIN PARTS

### 11. Undo the absorber pin from the cabinet
Use pliers to pinch the absorber pin’s protuberance, and knock the absorber pin out from back lightly; in the same way, remove the other one.

### 12. Undo the drum tub assembly
Undo the upper and façade counterweight   
I. Remove 3 screws fit on the upper counterweight and then pull out it.  
II. Remove 6 screws pull out the facade counterweight.  

Undo the panel support  
Remove two screws fixing the panel support, and then remove it.
