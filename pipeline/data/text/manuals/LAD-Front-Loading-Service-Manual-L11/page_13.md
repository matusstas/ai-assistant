# 5. MALFUNCTION CODES AND EXPLANATIONS

## E10
- **Check Procedure:**

  - Is the water tap is open?
    - **No** → Check the water tap and open it fully.
    - **Yes** →
      - Whether the inlet filter is blocked?
        - **Yes** → Clean the filter with the brush
        - **No** →
          - Whether the water inlet valve is well?
            - **No** → Change the water inlet valve
            - **Yes** →
              - Whether the water level sensor is well?
                - **No** → Change the water level sensor
                - **Yes** → Check the wire connector and harness before replacing PCB.
