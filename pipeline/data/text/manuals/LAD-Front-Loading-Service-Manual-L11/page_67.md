# 8 UNPACKING WAYS OF MAIN PARTS

## 9. Undo the drain pump
I. Pull out the terminal behind the drain pump.  
II. Nip out clamp between drain hose and the drain pump, and then pull out the drain hose.  
III. Loosen the screws fitted on the drain pump, and then pull out the drain pump.  

---

## 10. Undo the pulley and motor
**Undo the pulley**  
Rotate the pulley and at the same time pull out the belt.  

**Undo the motor**  
I. Let the machine lie down on the back and then pull out the motor wire and grounding wire.  
II. Use spanner to remove the motor screw, and lift up the motor with the other hand in case of falling to the drain pump.
